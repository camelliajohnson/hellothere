24 hours in Hong Kong.

Hong Kong or the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China, is an autonomous territory in China. Hong Kong was a British colony until 1997. Dubbed also as "Asia's World City", Hong Kong is known for its cosmopolitan outlook despite maintaining a strong individualistic culture. “Hong Kongers” are known for their fast-pace life but definitely know how to enjoy themselves! Indulge in Hong Kong’s finest tasty treats that will satiate your hunger and create memories at some of its most beautiful spots.
