Stockholm Cathedral

Beautiful, airy cathedral with light-colored materials, intricate brickwork, and a variety of artwork from the 14th century to modern times. The main highlight is the statue of St. George and the Dragon (1489) with details made of oak, elk antlers, and horsehair. Even if you aren't typically a church person, this is well worth checking out.
